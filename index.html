The Preamble

We the People of this holy order, united by our will to followeth the holy
and the burning passion to eradicate everything unholy,
taketh upon ourselves this quest of god,
and with the beliefs of Brotherhood and Sanctity,
embrace this constitution,
to organize and equip ourselves better in this war against the unholy,
under the name of The Holy Inquisition
