The Preamble

In the name of the Lord, I proclaim mine loyalty to the Holy Order of the Inquisition.
By my heart I swear loyalty to this Order and it's people, united by their will to square the unholy, so that the will of God prevails.
I declare mine fraternity and brotherhood to the people of this Holy Order, and taketh it upon myself to treat everyone with the dignity and respect worthy of a brother or sister.
I taketh the oath in the name of mine seniors, whomst I will closely followeth, but at each moment strive to be better under their guidance.
Oh Holy God, please shower Thy blessings upon Thy son/daughter, as I taketh on mine duty to square for Thy will.
Giveth me the power to the smite mine enemies with all mine strength on the battlefield, to giveth mine all to the cause of this Holy Order.
That I pray to Thee. Amen
